About TAP

The TAP class (also known as the Technology Adantage Program) is a program that students had the choice to join when going from grade 8 to grade 9 they had to apply and do interviews to join the program aslong as signing a contract only 24 people out of the 3 schools that were chosen were allowed to join the program. Know you maybe asking, "whats so special about this program". Well people in the TAP Program do some college work during their high school years and after their high school years they have a fully paid tution on 4 different IT Strands. After the 2 years of college they have a guaranteed interview With IBM
